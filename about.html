About Atlas Fuel

Unrivalled. Unmatched. Unstoppable.

Three words that capture the spirit of Atlas Fuel and the people who drive it forward every day.

Our Story

From a single station to a national fuel partner.

Since 2010, Atlas Fuel has grown from a single Western Australian service station into one of Australia's fastest-growing and most trusted fuel providers.

Over more than a decade, we've built a strong national network — serving mining, marine, agriculture, transport and retail with reliable bulk supply, logistics and on-site delivery. Driven by innovation and integrity, we anticipate customer needs, optimise logistics and build long-term partnerships through smart, efficient systems.

From our performance fleet to our world-class service stations, every step we take reflects a relentless drive to set new standards in the fuel industry. For our customers, our partners and our communities, Atlas Fuel is more than a brand — it's a promise of strength, progress and a future powered without limits.

Vision & Purpose

Our purpose is to provide reliable and affordable petroleum products to help create a better world for everyone.

By 2030 Atlas Fuel will be Australia's most trusted independent bulk-fuel partner — defined by safety performance, environmental discipline and the quality of every litre we deliver.

Milestones

A decade and a half of Australian fuel delivery.

2010

Founded in Kwinana, WA

A single independent service station opens. The green-and-white promise begins.

2014

Bulk diesel division launched

First mining-sector contracts signed; the bulk supply business is born.

2017

Heavy-vehicle accreditation

Fleet enters WA Heavy Vehicle Accreditation across fatigue, mass, maintenance and dimension.

2020

Marine bunkering capability

Barge-side bunkering services launched at the Port of Fremantle.

2022
