Our People

The crew behind every delivery.

Drivers, dispatchers, mechanics, marine operators and depot teams — Atlas Fuel is the sum of the people who turn the key every morning.

Our Team

Built on craft, care and a no-shortcuts culture.

From the Pilbara to the Port of Fremantle, Atlas Fuel is a team where pride in the job is expected — and visible in every clean truck, on-time delivery and signed safe-work method statement.

Our people are the difference between a fuel supplier and a fuel partner. They're the reason customers return contract after contract.

Operations

A round-the-clock dispatch and operations team.

Our 24/7 dispatch centre is the engine room. It's how a 02:00 emergency top-up turns into a fuelled-up shift starting at 06:00.

  • Driver allocation, fatigue management and compliance
  • Customer service team with named account managers
